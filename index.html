Intro

As a recent Master's graduate in Business Analytics from Syracuse University, I have a deep passion for using data to drive business success. Over the past three years, I've honed my skills in deciphering complex datasets and transforming them into actionable insights that lead to profitable outcomes.

In my previous roles, I've led various data-driven projects that optimized operations and provided significant financial benefits. Whether developing advanced analytics models, creating comprehensive dashboards, or presenting critical insights to stakeholders, I've always aimed to foster a culture of data-driven decision-making. My blend of technical expertise and business acumen has been key to delivering strategic solutions that drive improvements and cost savings.

I'm particularly passionate about solving intricate business problems with data and am committed to continuous learning and innovation in the field of analytics. I'm eager to bring my skills and experience to a forward-thinking organization where I can contribute to impactful and transformative projects. I'm excited about the opportunity to apply my expertise to drive strategic decisions, enhance operational efficiency, and support the growth and success of the organization.
